Metroexpress

Sub title

metroexpress is a complementary pilot ride-sharing feeder service that connect specific service zones around Metro stations. The service uses a fleet of branded Mercedes Vito vans, seating 7 passengers per vehicle. However, seating is currently restricted to 3 passengers per ride, due to COVID precautions.

This service offers greater flexibility and convenience than a conventional bus service as customers can request metroexpress on-demand. Metroexpress service is exclusive for registered Metro passengers and can only be used in conjunction with a metro journey. Customers can book a ride through the metroexpre
